About

I'm a Senior Software Engineer specializing in front-end development, with experience in full-stack development. Started in the field of mechatronics, where I won two silver medals in IEEE robotics competitions in Latin America and hold a patent for a soil analysis automation tool. Today I have migrated to the field of modern web engineering. This precision engineering mindset is systems.

For the last several years I've focused on high-stakes frontend architecture: Migrating a large-scale platform at Oliver Wyman, leading a small team at Imobanco on a responsive financial app, and shipping a multilingual analytics platform for SMEs at Friday Finance (acquired by Pliant in 2023). I specialize in Vue 2 → 3 migrations, monolith → microfrontends and in performance engineering with Islands Architecture and Core Web Vitals.

I care less about specific frameworks and more about how UI, state, schemas and backend fit together into a predictable system. Outside of work I cook, run, and am slowly learning game development with Godot.
